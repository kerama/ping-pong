Rules:

Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions as such:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 becomes "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 becomes "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 becomes "pingpong"
